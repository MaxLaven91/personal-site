myJet2

Jet2’s existing customer account was an underutilised and outdated offering, designed primarily for flight bookings. It lacked engagement, was rarely maintained, and provided little long-term value to users beyond storing flight details. The challenge was to redefine myJet2 as a valuable digital touchpoint, leveraging it as a marketing opportunity to onboard new users and offer personalised experiences.
